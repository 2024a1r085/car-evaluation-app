How the Model Works

This tool uses a decision tree classifier trained on the UCI Car Evaluation dataset to rate a car based on 6 features:

  • Buying Price — the upfront cost of the car
  • Maintenance Cost — estimated ongoing upkeep
  • Number of Doors — how many doors it has
  • Passenger Capacity — how many people it seats
  • Luggage Boot Size — small, medium, or large
  • Safety Rating — low, medium, or high

Based on these inputs, the model outputs one of four ratings:

  • UNACCEPTABLE — not worth considering
  • ACCEPTABLE — meets minimum standards
  • GOOD — a solid choice
  • VERY GOOD — highly recommended
